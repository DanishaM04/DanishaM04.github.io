Hours/Direction

From downtown, take #38 Geary Boulevard to Park Presidio and transfer to #28 northbound to get to the Golden Gate Bridge. If you are taking Golden Gate Transit from San Francisco, the #10, #70 or #80 lines will take you to the bridge.
